# Product Context

PayPortal is a merchant onboarding and operations portal.

## Merchant Experience

- Register and sign in securely.
- Submit company, contact, and address information.
- Upload KYC documents and track reviewer notes and approval status.
- Generate and rotate sandbox or production API credentials after approval.

## Admin Experience

- Monitor onboarding statistics and recent activity.
- Search and inspect merchant applications.
- Review KYC evidence.
- Approve, reject, or request additional documents with an audit trail.

## Roles

- `Merchant`: owns one merchant application and its related data.
- `Admin`: reviews all applications and manages approval decisions.
