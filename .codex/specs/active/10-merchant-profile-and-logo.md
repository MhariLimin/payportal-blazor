# 10. Merchant Profile and Logo

## Goal

Complete merchant onboarding and profile ownership workflows.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Registration clearly marks required fields.
- Business type and industry use standard dropdown choices.
- Merchants can edit their own company, contact, and address details.
- Merchants can upload a PNG or JPEG company logo.
- The company logo appears in the profile, application lists, and signed-in
  merchant account menu.
- Profile changes create specific activity records.
