# 15 Feedback 3 Review Actions

## Goal

Make review actions more granular and make Compliance Review independent from
final approval.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Admins can verify individual KYC documents.
- Admins can reject individual KYC documents with notes.
- Compliance Review can be completed separately from application approval.
- Verification milestones are ordered as profile, documents, compliance review,
  and approval.
- Document requirement statuses use readable labels instead of symbols.

## Status

Resolved.
