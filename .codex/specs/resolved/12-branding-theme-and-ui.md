# 12. Branding, Theme, and UI

## Goal

Apply the PayPortal identity and improve the visual quality and usability of
the Blazor interface.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The supplied `payportal.png` brand artwork is integrated into the app.
- Light and dark modes are available and persist in the browser.
- Theme controls and logout live in a user-menu dropdown.
- The placeholder global search is removed and replaced by useful context.
- Labels and activity text use readable title casing.
- Typography, spacing, cards, tables, forms, and responsive states are polished.
- Existing pages remain usable on desktop and mobile layouts.
