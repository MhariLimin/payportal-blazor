# 16 Feedback 3 Activity, Notifications, and Audit

## Goal

Expose activity more clearly for admins and merchants.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Admins can filter activity by merchant, actor, action type, and date range.
- Merchants have a notification center scoped to their own merchant activity.
- Merchant dashboards and notifications do not show other merchants' activity.
- Merchant profiles expose an application audit log.
- Activity entries cover document review and compliance-review events.

## Status

Resolved.
