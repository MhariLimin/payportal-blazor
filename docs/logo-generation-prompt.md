# PayPortal Logo Generation Prompt

Use the following prompt with an image-generation or design tool:

```text
Create a polished, original brand logo for an application named "PayPortal".

Product context:
PayPortal is a secure merchant onboarding and payment-operations portal used by
business merchants and internal administrators. Merchants register their
companies, submit business and contact information, upload KYC verification
documents, track application approval status, review compliance feedback, and
generate secure API credentials after approval. Administrators monitor merchant
statistics, review onboarding applications, assess risk, request additional
documents, approve or reject applications, and audit recent activity.

Core brand attributes:
- Secure, trustworthy, compliant, and professional
- Modern financial technology without looking like a generic bank
- Efficient onboarding and controlled access
- Clear connection between merchants, payments, verification, and a digital
  portal
- Suitable for a credible B2B SaaS product and an interview/demo portfolio

Required logo concept:
- Include a distinct portal icon embedded directly into the logo mark, not as a
  separate decorative element.
- The portal should read visually as a doorway, gateway, opening, or controlled
  digital entry point.
- Integrate the portal shape with a subtle letter "P" so the symbol can work as
  a standalone application icon.
- Optionally use one restrained secondary motif such as a shield edge, check
  mark, transaction path, or connected node to communicate verification and
  secure payment access.
- Keep the symbol simple enough to remain recognizable at favicon and sidebar
  sizes.

Visual direction:
- Minimal geometric vector style
- Clean negative space and balanced proportions
- Rounded corners that feel approachable but still enterprise-grade
- Primary palette: cyan, teal, and deep slate
- Suggested colors: cyan #06B6D4, teal #0D9488, deep slate #0F172A, white
- A subtle cyan-to-teal gradient is acceptable inside the mark, but the logo
  must also work in flat single-color black and white
- Use a modern sans-serif wordmark with "PayPortal" written exactly with both
  capital Ps
- Give "Pay" and "Portal" balanced visual weight; do not make the logo resemble
  PayPal or any existing payment brand

Composition deliverables:
1. Primary horizontal logo: portal-P symbol on the left and PayPortal wordmark
   on the right
2. Standalone square application icon using the same embedded portal-P symbol
3. Monochrome light and dark variants
4. Transparent background
5. Generous clear space and no mockup environment

Avoid:
- Credit cards, dollar signs, coins, bank buildings, handshakes, shopping carts,
  padlocks as the main symbol, or overly literal payment imagery
- Complex illustrations, tiny details, 3D rendering, photorealism, mascots,
  glossy effects, excessive shadows, or busy backgrounds
- Copying the visual identity, typography, double-P structure, or color
  arrangement of PayPal or another known fintech company

Output:
Present the logo as crisp, centered vector-style artwork on a transparent
background. Prioritize a unique, memorable portal-shaped P mark that clearly
communicates secure entry into merchant onboarding and payment operations.
```
